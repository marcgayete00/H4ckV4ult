rsync (TCP/873)

rsync (remote sync) is a utility for transferring and synchronizing files between a computer and a storage drive and across networked computers by comparing the modification times and sizes of files

Usage

    Accessing shares

      nc -nv 127.0.0.1 873

      Once insede you must type #list in order to list all shares

      In case we would like to check what content is inside a share we could perform

      rsync -av --list-only rsync://127.0.0.1/$sharename
